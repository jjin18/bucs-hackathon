# Leadership (Evidence)

Topic: leadership

At age seventeen, Jiahui organized a student-led policy campaign in Canada during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The campaign focused on grading policies affecting students during major educational disruptions. Many students felt existing grading systems did not fairly reflect learning conditions during lockdowns and remote schooling.

Jiahui launched a petition advocating for policy changes that would account for these extraordinary circumstances. The campaign quickly gained momentum and collected more than fifteen thousand signatures.

The initiative received national media coverage and brought attention to fairness in educational assessment during pandemic disruptions.

This campaign demonstrated early experience in grassroots organizing, public advocacy, and navigating institutional policy systems.
