# Virality (Evidence)

Topic: virality

Some of Jiahui's projects and cultural experiments have spread widely online and attracted media coverage.

One example is the Performative Male Contest held in San Francisco. The event was inspired by internet discussions around the idea of the "performative male," a term used online to describe men who adopt progressive aesthetics or behaviors as social signaling.

The contest turned the meme into a real-world event where participants exaggerated stereotypical traits associated with performative masculinity. The satirical nature of the event made it highly shareable on social media and sparked discussion about internet culture and authenticity.

The event received media coverage and circulated widely online.

In addition to media coverage, some of Jiahui's LinkedIn posts have reached large audiences and generated substantial engagement.
