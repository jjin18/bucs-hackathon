# Work Experience (Evidence)

Topic: work_experience

Jiahui Jin works at the intersection of healthcare technology, artificial intelligence, and consumer product systems.

Her work focuses on translating emerging AI capabilities into real products that interact with users in sensitive environments such as healthcare.

At NimbleRx, she worked on consumer-facing health AI systems designed to help patients navigate prescription management and pharmacy communication.

One of the major initiatives is **Mira**, an AI health companion she launched at NimbleRx — it reached **over 1 million patients**. She worked on consumer-facing health AI systems (prescription management, pharmacy communication, medication workflows) and ran LLM safety evaluation on 10K+ medical messages across 20+ eval cycles.

Working on consumer health AI exposed the complexities of building systems where trust, reliability, and safety directly affect user adoption. The work involved understanding how patients interpret AI-generated responses and what signals make users comfortable acting on AI advice.

Rather than treating AI purely as a technical system, her work emphasizes product design, behavioral signals, and human trust in AI systems operating in high-stakes contexts.
